High Ground VR

Currently in Development

Track Progress Here

  • Overview

    High Ground VR is a virtual reality tower defense game built in Unity3D. This aim of this project is to explore how the player can deal with seemingly intelligent enemies adapting their strategy to compete against the player. It has a top-down 'building' mode, in which the player can place buildings and plan routes, and a FPS-style 'action' mode, where the player can shoot various spells to aid in the defense.
