  • Overview

    HYPERTEC is a voxel style dogfighter set in space. It features modular ships and procedural weapon & engines. We are developing this under team Backyard Giant, with our development being focused on finding a mid-point between the movement of a fighter jet and a spaceship in zero gravity, with the goal of achieving a really satisfying movement system.

    I am the Design Lead on this project, in charge of: asset production, user experience, usability testing and game branding. I also started this project with writing a research paper in game user interfaces and how they can be designed to achieve cognitive flow. HYPERTEC is an ongoing project, and is being built in unity for use with a controller
