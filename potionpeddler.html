  • Overview

    Potion Peddler is a fantasy themed idle clicker game made for Android. Users have to swipe a randomly generated sequence within the given time to satisfy customers orders, and move onto the next customer. Gold is awarded for quick service, and combos are available to increase profit! This is the second project i've worked on that was developed for mobile, after Rhythm Fun.

    It was developed as a summer project between stage two and stage three of University. The art assets were sourced from "Humble Fantasy Game Dev Bundle" which was released on 19th February 2019.
