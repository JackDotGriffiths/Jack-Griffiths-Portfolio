  • Overview

    Contrelement is an elemental based tower defense game made in 72 hours for the Geta Game Jam 9! The theme for this game jam was "Contrasting Elements".The goal is to protect yourself from the incoming enemies using your minions. Spawn the minions of the opposing element to have advantage in battle! Destroy all of the enemy spawning portals to complete the level!

    Contrelement was a team game jam that we completed during the summer break, and due to having a team of 4 we were able to complete a relatively polished project. As with many game jams, there weren't any defined roles during the development, however the tasks I completed focused on general User Experience. This meant I made things like camera shake, particle effects, and not to mention making all of the sound effects with my mouth!
